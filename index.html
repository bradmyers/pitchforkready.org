Five Core Principles

The structural pillars of our movement. Non-negotiable, non-partisan, and absolutely necessary.

05
gavel

Abolish Citizens United

End the era where money is considered free speech. Corporations are not people; their influence must be decoupled from the ballot box.

visibility_off

Abolish The Patriot Act

Restore the fundamental right to privacy. Systemic surveillance is the antithesis of a free and open society.

trending_up

No Individual Stock Ownership

Congress and their families must be barred from individual stock ownership to eliminate conflicts of interest in policy making.

map

Abolish Gerrymandering

Districts must be drawn by independent commissions. Voters should choose their politicians, not the other way around.

security

Prevent Insurrections

Strengthen legal frameworks to protect the peaceful transfer of power and ensure accountability for those who threaten democratic stability.

The Candidate Pledge

Our goal is 100% buy-in from candidates who agree to implement these 5 actions as their top 5 priorities. We believe these core structural changes are the fundamental path to fixing the country.
